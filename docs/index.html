Urban cars

Our focus is on urban cars. Cities are the environment where the electric vehicles perform the best and where thermal cars perform the worst. In an urban environment the electric car consumption is very low allowing to have small batteries and lighter vehicles.

Robustness

Classic motors are complex, fragile and often the cause of breakdown while the structure of the vehicle can still run for a while. Electric motors can handle a million miles thanks to a simple and efficient design.

Out of emissions norms

While the restrictions for diesel and gas cars increase, limiting their access in cities, the conversion releases your car from any restriction and reaches your car to the top of the standards.

Broken cars

Many cars with a broken motor are sold for peanuts while they are still valuable. By replacing their motorisation they can find back their original value and even more.

Green transition

Circular Economy

Converting the car does not only bring a better experience of riding, it also saves obsolete cars from the trash. One car converted is one car less produced, saving a lot of grey energy and GHG emissions necessary for manufacture. This is pure circular economy.
